Fried Steak

Description

A perfect dish for a protein and taste-rich lunch that is simple to cook!

Ingredients

  • Beef steak (ribeye, sirloin, or preferred cut)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Vegetable oil or butter

Optional:

  • Garlic cloves
  • Fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary

Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

  1. Pat steak dry with paper towels.
  2. Season generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  3. Heat a skillet over high heat, add oil or butter.
  4. Place steak in skillet, sear 2â€“4 minutes per side depending on thickness and desired doneness.
  5. Add garlic and herbs near the end for extra flavor (optional).
  6. Let steak rest 5 minutes before slicing or serving.
