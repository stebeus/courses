Lasagna

Description

A special dish reserved for special days of the week.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (fresh or dried)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano and basil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs (for mixing with ricotta)

Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer 15–20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and a bit of Parmesan.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C or 375°F).
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
  8. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, then mozzarella, then meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  9. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  10. Bake 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
