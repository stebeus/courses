Greek Salad

Description

A great and mythical starting-point for your breakfasts and lunch accompaniments.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt (season it by taste)
  • Black pepper

Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

  1. Wash and chop cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, and green bell pepper into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place vegetables in a large bowl.
  3. Add Kalamata olives.
  4. Cut feta cheese into cubes or crumble it on top.
  5. Drizzle olive oil and vinegar (or lemon juice).
  6. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  7. Toss gently and serve fresh.
